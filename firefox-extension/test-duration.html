Duration Settings in Extension

How to Configure:
  1. Open extension settings
  2. Scroll to "Animation Duration" section
  3. Choose your preferred duration
  4. Save settings
  5. Test on your configured websites

Duration Effects

⚡ Short: Quick celebrations (2x faster fade) - Perfect for quick acknowledgments
⏱️ Medium: Balanced timing (default) - Standard fireworks experience
🎬 Long: Extended celebration (2x slower fade) - Great for major achievements
